Hello, this is the index/demo/doc page for CanvasTransitions. Below you will find examples of the transitions and how to call them whatever.

Useful Information:

A fill is used to cover the screen. A clear is used to show the page.

All transitions called on this page, will clear 1 second after the fill is complete, with a generic clear.

Any Transition can also be a "clear", by simply passing "clear" as an argument.

You can mix different fill's and clear's to create your unique transition.
